About Us

A place that serves staff and student needs. Vegetarian and non-vegetarian food is available at the Epic Cafe. The Canteen is a reasonably-priced, self-service canteen. Hot/Cold Beverage counters are provided in the canteen extension.

You are requested to stand in queue when paying for your food tokens or collecting food/beverages. Dispose off waste appropriately in the bins kept for this purpose.
